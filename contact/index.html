Before writing

What makes a good first message?

A good first message does not need to be long. It should be specific enough to understand the goal, timing, and whether the work fits.

  • Project type and expected outcome
  • Reference links or existing materials
  • Deadline, launch window, or urgency
  • Budget range or collaboration model
  • Preferred contact route and timezone
