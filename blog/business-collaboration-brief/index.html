What to include

A useful first message includes the project type, expected outcome, deadline, budget range, reference links, and the decision maker or contact person.

Why scope matters

Small creator projects are easiest to finish when the scope is explicit. A clear scope keeps the work reviewable, prevents hidden assumptions, and makes later maintenance easier.
