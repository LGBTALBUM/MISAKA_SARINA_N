Why this site became a dark archive

A future note about turning a simple link page into a music-first creator archive.

Body

Context

The previous homepage was useful as a link collection, but it did not fully represent the music catalogue, project identity, or long-term archive needs of Misaka Sarina / Siacone Art.

Design direction

The Dark Archive direction keeps the site quiet, music-first, and slightly technical. It avoids a generic link-in-bio feeling and makes room for releases, works, notes, and maintenance traces.
